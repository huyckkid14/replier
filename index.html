What it does

Choose how Replier handles incoming Gmail.

Replier is designed around explicit modes, visible settings, and draft-first workflows so email automation stays understandable.

T

Send a saved template

Use a quick acknowledgement, a busy reply, or your own custom template for trusted senders.

D

Draft and notify

Create Gmail drafts for unread inbox messages, then review them yourself before anything goes out.

AI

Generate concise replies

Draft replies with your selected tone and optional signature, routed through a Cloudflare Worker to OpenAI.

5

Poll unread inbox email

Check unread Gmail inbox messages on a five-minute extension alarm or manually with Poll now.

@

Trust specific senders

Limit auto-send behavior to senders you trust. Everyone else can be converted into a draft instead.

!

Surface drafts needing attention

Blocked, declined, pending, and error drafts appear in the popup with badge counts and draft links.

Safety review

Reviewed auto-send only sends when every check passes.

The extension can create a draft instead of sending when it sees risk signals, review failures, untrusted senders, or processing errors.

  • Local safety checker Blocks placeholders, unsupported personal claims, wrong signatures, no-reply requests, and promotional email signals.
  • Three independent AI reviewers Each reviewer evaluates the original email and proposed draft. One block is enough to stop sending.
  • Draft-only fallbacks When review fails, a sender is not trusted, an error occurs, or the original asks for no reply, Replier saves a draft instead.
  • Automatic disclosure Generated outbound messages can include a disclosure that the email was automatically generated by Replier.
